ABOUT ME

Hi! Kenneth here. I'm currently an undergrad at UC San Diego studying Computer Science. I've also contributed to a research project at MIT CSAIL, but that's for another time.

Currently, my interests span from Artificial Intelligence, specifically how AI can be used to solve global or social challenges without infringing the data privacy of individual citizens, and Human-Computer Interaction, applying UI/UX principles, how could humans more effectively and efficiently utilize computational technologies and the data they create to accomplish complex tasks.

I was born in NorCal, grew up in China, and glowed up in SoCal. I got into programming (C Language, specifically) in 7th grade, but didn't start seriously self-teaching until high school (Web Dev, this time), after finally gaining my parents' support.
