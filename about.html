ABOUT ME

Hi! Kenneth here. I'm a first-year undergrad at UC San Diego studying Computer Science.

My academic interest resides in solving problems at the intersection between technology, society, individuals, and the economy. How to make machine learning algorithms less prone to human biases? How to fix issues with our "Web 2.0" dominated by profit-hungry, data-seeking Internet giants? How can we further empower data — not for the sake of convenience, but humanity?

I don't have a definite answer to those questions (tbh they're pretty broad), but because of them, at least I know why I want to enrich myself through higher education.

Other than that... I'm enjoying Christ, learning to produce music, getting better at volleyball, reading, and attempting to cook good ramen for people I care about.
